    Credit cards are mandatory for the security deposit. This blocked amount is not deducted from the card unless there are charges added to a contract after the vehicle has been returned. For example, Salik (Toll) & traffic fines.

    The security deposit is not charged to the card so there is no actual refund. However, it can take up to 29 days (or fewer, depending on the card provider or bank) for the block to be released. After that the customer can use the blocked amount again.

    Customers might get a message from their bank when the money is blocked. The blocked amount won’t be debited from the account. Customers will only see actual charges on their credit card statements.

    Parking in UAE is readily available. Parking along the roadside is paid parking with parking meters installed. Parking charges vary by time of the day and parking zone. Customers need to check the parking zone they are using and pay the appropriate fee.
